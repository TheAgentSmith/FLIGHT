Detailed Description

A slab of the world's 3D space maintained by the CollisionManager.

Sector maintains lists of weak references to StaticSolids and DynamicSolids. Whenever these lists change, it rebuilds a vector containing the complete set of all pairs of dynamic solids, and a set of all pairs of dynamic solids and static solids. The sector does not generate pairs for static solids, because they do not move and it may be assumed that they will not collide to do any motion of their own.
